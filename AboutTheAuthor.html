About The Author

Kyle Esguerra

Greetings! I am Kyle Noah D. Esguerra, a student from 9-pinatubo, the creator of The Vision. I have a lot of interests, including sports, books, ut one of the most dominant interests I have is television. That is precisely the reason for creating this magazine. I created The Vision to show my love for television. After we all went into quarantine due to covid, I found myself lost. But TV found me. Yes, that might be silly to hear but I started loving it. It started out as a casual hobby. It was something to do out of boredom. Then, I fell in love with certain shows. That was when I really realized that "wow, people can make so substantial but so entertaining shows". Watching television, makes me ponder if I can engage in a field like that when I grow up. For now, I can show my love through this magazine.

The Vision (which obviously stands for TV when abbreviated) is not just a project. It's something I made because it's a way for me to express my love for these shows. A way for me to introduce you a bit to what my world feels like. It's a way for me to introduce you to the extraordinary possibilities the silver screen can take you. I hope you enjoy this magazine!
